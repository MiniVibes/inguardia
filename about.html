About Us

InGuardia AI is a cutting-edge cybersecurity company founded by Omer Sacks and Or Zisserman. We specialize in AI-powered insider threat detection and response systems. Our mission is to provide enterprises with advanced tools to protect their critical data and systems from internal threats. Our innovative platform uses machine learning and behavioral analytics to proactively identify and neutralize potential threats, ensuring the safety and integrity of your organization.

Our core features include real-time monitoring, behavioral analytics, automated response, and comprehensive reporting. We are committed to continuous improvement and innovation to stay ahead of emerging threats and provide the highest level of security for our clients.
